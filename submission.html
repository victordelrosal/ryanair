Abstract

This project delivers a comprehensive Business Intelligence dashboard for Ryanair's board-level decision-making, analyzing airline operational and financial performance using publicly available data. The dashboard synthesizes key performance indicators including yield management (+21% average fare increase in Q1 FY2026), load factor optimization (95%), fuel hedging strategy (85% hedged at $76/bbl), and network profitability analysis across 2,400+ routes. Using data from Ryanair's quarterly earnings reports, regulatory filings, and industry benchmarks, the analysis reveals five critical insights: (1) profit rebound driven by yield strength (â‚¬820M Q1 profit, +128% YoY), (2) cost leadership secured through fuel hedging, (3) geographic concentration with Italy, Spain, and UK generating >50% revenue, (4) strategic network optimization cutting low-margin markets, and (5) competitive advantages from rivals' operational challenges. The interactive dashboard enables scenario planning, risk assessment, and data-driven strategic decisions. Technologies used include JavaScript for visualizations (Canvas API), HTML/CSS for responsive design, and GitHub Pages for deployment. The project demonstrates how business intelligence transforms raw airline data into actionable board-level insights supporting profit maximization and strategic growth.
